Chicken Quesadilla

Another fantastic meal that is low in calories and high in protein

This meal is incredibly simple to make. It should take you only 15 minutes to make, and is absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/3 cup, shredded low-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup lettuce shredded
  • 1/3 cup salsa
  • 1 tbsp reduced-fat sour cream
  • 2 whole whole wheat soft tortillas

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine cooked chicken, salsa and lettuce.
  2. Spread the sour cream on one tortilla, then top with the chicken mixture.
  3. Sprinkle with cheddar and top with the remaining tortilla.
  4. In a large skillet coated with cooking spray, over low heat, cook the quesadilla until light brown, usually about 3 minutes each side. Turn carefully with a wide spatula.
