Anabolic French Toast

A delicious high-protein breakfast meal to prepare you for an intense day of training!

Ordinary french toast is not quite the best breakfast meal if you're looking to pack on muscle. It is often prepared with tons of sugar and cream. With just a few tweaks, you can reduce the calories and increase the protein, all while still making a delightful breakfast!

Also, this is a VERY simple recipe. There's a grand total of six ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Protein Powder
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Start by whisking together all the ingredients (except for the bread of course). Once combined, dip each slice of bread in the mixture until fully coated
  2. Add cooking spray onto a non-stick pan and place it over medium heat. Once hot, add the soaked bread and cook for 6-7 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  3. Repeat this process until all the bread has been cooked.
  4. Serve immediately!
